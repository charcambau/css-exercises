We Love Green

Who are we?

Sure, cheese is great and all but wait to see what we can offer...

All we ask from you is a bit of trust. We'll take care of the rest. Here, we make sure our customers get delicious food from quality products. And, we do it with a smile!

You're a foodie? Good. Us too. And we're passionate about serving you tasty food that'll make you forget there's no cheese in it. That's our promise.

Why are we doing this?

Well, there's 3 reasons :

  1. We care for the environment, and want to help. It's bad (even National Geographic says so.), and if we can make it slightly better we will!
  2. We want to make sure our customers eat healthy food, from good products and loving preparation.
  3. And lastly, now that we have a restaurant, our life evolves around food and that's pretty cool...
